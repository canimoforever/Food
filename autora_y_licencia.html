Título Food
Descripción In this lesson, you will learn topic-specific vocabulary on the subject of food (meat, fish, vegetables and fruit) and ways of cooking. You will revise the pronunciation of adjectives ending in -ed and finally you will have to read a text on some restaurant reviews in addition to some speaking practice about food.
