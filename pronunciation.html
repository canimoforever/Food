Pronunciation

Listen and repeat

Listen to the pronunciation of the -ed ending in the following words and repeat.

boiled

roasted

grilled

fried

baked

Remember:

The final -ed is pronounced /d/ after voiced consonants and vowels, /t/ after voiceless consonants and /id/ after the sounds /t/ or /d/.
